Geoffrey Gichana Guto(Tripple G) Tribute

G.G Life History

GGG was born at kiareni, marani subcounty,kitutu chache north constituency, kisii county, in the year 1952. He was a staunch christian & believer in the catholic faith.

GG attended kericho Chagaiki primary school and proceeded to Bware secondary school for his o level studies

He was the son of the late Gichana Gichana and the late Maria Kerubo from Kiareni village, Marani division, Mwagichana location. He is brother to Charles Nyakundi,Sebastian Sindani,Agnes Ntabo,late Dominic Ochwangi,late Fredrick Mirera,Tumbo, late Mongina, late Mwango,late Nyambori,Nyangau,late Nyanchoka,late Gilbert Orango and late Cleophas Ngote.

Husband to Josphine Nyaboke Okemwa, Father to Edwin Gichana of Kisii county,Maureen Moindi of ministry of Education Brenda kwamboka of East African community and Brian siro.

GG secured a job opportunity in the suburbs of Nairobi as a finger print technician, he was then absorbed into the Ministry of Eduacation at jogoo house, he served in differnt departments among them, grants dept and promoted to office superitendant,he was later transfered to the Ministry of Cooperatives working at reinsurance plaza,where he retired. He lived partly in Nairobi and his ancestral home Kiareni until his untimely demise on fatal road accident involving a bodaboda and a vehicle on his way home.

Geoffrey was happily married with his weded spouse Josphine
